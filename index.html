Our Story
At Tissomi, we exist for one reason. To make fabric sourcing simpler, smarter, and more reliable for garment manufacturers.

We know that finding the right fabric is one of the hardest parts of garment manufacturing. Too often, manufacturers are forced to choose between quality and price, or spend weeks chasing the right supplier. We built Tissomi to change that.

We are a focused, reliable supplier of high quality womenswear fabrics. Compact cotton, rayon, lyocell, modal, and distinctive synthetic weaves, curated specifically for manufacturers who refuse to compromise. Every fabric in our range is chosen for its hand feel, finish, and consistency, so you always know what you're getting.

Our promise is simple. The right fabric, at the right price, every time.
