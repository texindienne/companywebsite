Designs
Discover an array of exquisite designs, showcasing the artistry of Jacquard, the intricacy of Embroidery, and the sophistication of Dobby. Explore diverse fabrics, from fine polyester and breathable cotton to the natural textures of viscose and linen.
Identity
Our unwavering commitment to exceptional fabric designs draws inspiration from the spirit of industry pioneers like John Kay and Edmund Cartwright. Much like Kay's flying shuttle and Cartwright's power loom revolutionized weaving, we are driven to reshape the industry with our distinctive fabric designs.

Just as these inventors triggered social and economic changes, we at K&C strive to create a transformative impact on the world of fabrics. Our commitment to quality and innovation reflects the spirit of K&C, ensuring that their legacy lives on in every meticulously crafted piece.
